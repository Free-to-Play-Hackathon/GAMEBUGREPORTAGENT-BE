# Phase 10 - Trust Hardening

## Muc tieu

Lam sau MVP neu con thoi gian. Phase nay bien Trust Gate MVP thanh trust engine day du hon.

## Scope

- Source group identity va independence model.
- Corroboration dua tren independent source groups.
- Material/blocking conflict model va human resolution co actor/reason/audit.
- Versioned confidence calculator voi breakdown.
- Quality dimensions chi tiet: required coverage, provenance coverage, direct-step rate, conflict/unknown penalties.
- Partial-result matrix day du cho provider/search/reranker/game-context failures.
- Provider fallback va circuit breaker.
- Quality-based model escalation, neu du budget va model available.
- Redaction/minimum-context/retention hardening.
- Provider execution, redaction event, provenance validation run va quality report persistence day du.
- Revalidation/reprocess voi policy moi, khong rewrite run cu.

## Exit gate

Trust hardening chi dong khi corroboration/conflict/fallback/retention co regression tests va khong thay doi am tham human decisions da commit.
